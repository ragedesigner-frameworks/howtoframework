Key Concepts & Terminology

What is Framework Generation vs Framework Consumption?
Framework Consumption: Learning and applying existing frameworks created by others (Six Sigma, Agile, Design Thinking). Results in rigid application and context mismatch.

Framework Generation: Building custom frameworks from your own expertise that fit your specific context, workflow, and challenges. Results in natural adoption and systematic improvement.
What is Systematic Thinking?
Systematic thinking is the learnable capability to identify patterns in successful problem-solving and structure them into reusable frameworks. RageDesigner's position: systematic thinking is teachable technology, not innate talent.
What is the Decision Economy?

What is Force Multiplier Effect?
Framework libraries create compound advantage. Each framework built makes the next framework easier to create, generating exponential rather than linear returns on systematic thinking investment.
